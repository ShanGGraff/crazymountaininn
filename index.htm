estled between the Crazies, Castle, and Little Belt Mountains, Martinsdale, Montana is a small town teeming with history. In the middle of it all is the charming Crazy Mountain Inn. Owned and operated by Peter and Cheryl Marchi, the Crazy Mountain Inn is perfect for hikers, hunters, fishermen, and nature seekers alike. With beautiful turn-of-the-century accommodations, you are sure to leave with the same small town smile you were greeted with!
